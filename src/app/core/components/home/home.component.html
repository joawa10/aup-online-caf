Humble beginnings

ABOUT PUC HEALTH FOODS

Started in 1939, the school decides to promote biblical healthy diet and begins serving only vegetarian dishes in the caferia. This initiates the development of the food factory later known as the PUC Health Foods

AUP Health Foods creates exceptional vegetarian products and variety of pastries such as pineapple and coconut cookies, yema cakes, choco loaf, variety of hopia flavors, terin-terin, and many more. The loafs, cookies and soya milk have been cited as the students’s favorite choice of delicacy when satisfying their hungry souls. All vegetarian products are made in-house and delivered to your house so you can enjoy cooking it at home. Our commitment to quality is never compromised.
